## Why another AI plugin?

I tried most of the AI plugins avaiable to Neovim (at least the most famous ones), and I didn't like some parts of each one.

- [Avante](https://github.com/yetone/avante.nvim): Feels i little too big and complicated, I want somthing more simple and lightweight.
- [Code Companion](https://github.com/olimorris/codecompanion.nvim): Feels I little complicated for what I want.
- [sidekick](https://github.com/folke/sidekick.nvim): Too much focused in `github-copilot`, but closer to what I want.
- [agentic](https://github.com/carlos-algms/agentic.nvim): Honestly, I probably would be fine using this, is really close to what I want, but also, will be more fun to do it myself, so here it is.

## Why vibe coded?

Because the goal right now is to explore and experiment, not to engineer. I never developed a Neovim plugin neither developed in Lua, so vibe code feels rigth to explore this idea.

## Will it be only vibe coded?

Not forever. Once the core ideas solidify and the project finds its shape, it'll get proper structure, refactoring, and maybe even tests. But for now, vibes.
